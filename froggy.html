Introduction to Froggy

Froggy is an independant website project developed by Touseef Hossain with the intent of learning how to utilize HTML and CSS to make websites (he picked up on some Javascript and PHP too). As an added bonus, he wished to learn more about frogs and how to take care of them as a pet, and thought this was a great opportunity to multitask. Why frogs, specifically? Well, it's a not-so-funny story involving coworker talk on break time (ie. the reason will remain unknown).

This simple website consists of multiple webpages that discuss different aspects of domestic frog knowledge, and also contains a page dedicated to getting into contact with Touseef (in case anyone has a question, complaint, gripe, critique, statement of dissatisfaction/annoyance/protest/objection, etc.). This website may not contain the most information on domestic frog care but it was designed with the intent to ensure the visuals are easy on the eyes and the website itself is easy to navigate through. Once you are done admiring the beauty that is this home page, scroll back up to the top and click one of the links to go to another beautiful page with different content residing in them!

Below are some pictures of frogs with their names shown if you hover your mouse over them. These are some of the frogs you are able to adopt as a pet. Try clicking on them to learn more about how to take care of them. But if you don't know much about frogs yet, it's better to scroll back up and click the "Get Started" button.
